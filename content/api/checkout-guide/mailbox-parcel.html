Pakke i postkassen in checkout

1 Delivery option Pakke i Postkassen

Pakke i postkassen is recommended default shipping option for small, lower value shipments, as it is the preferred delivery method for recipients for these kind of items.

Mailbox parcel are slower than pickup point and home delivery, are uninsured and do not support ID control, age / social control, cooling or other special services. For deliveries needing these extra services, use Home Delivery or Pickup Point.

4 Pose på dør

As mailboxes vary in sizes, the maximum size of Package to Mailbox will not fit in every mailbox. To accommodate this, it is recommended to provide an option in checkout for the Delivery to doorstep extra service, as a fallback solution.

If the user ticks off and then agrees to terms, the mail carrier can leave packages that do not fit in the mailbox in a bag left at the door, instead of sending it to the nearest pickup point. When the mail carrier activates bag on door, the delivery will be given an additional charge. However, this is still cheaper and faster than re-routing to pick up location.

Delivery to doorstep should also include a free textfield to provide an apartment number or description, to help the mail carrier make sure the package is left in a bag at the correct door.

Add the property unit number on the label

Adding the property unit number to the label will help ensuring that the package will be delivered as a bag on door. We recommend adding this information to the label, either in the second address line, or in the additional information section (additionalAdressInfo).

Property unit number … … the unit number indicates which flat in the building you live in. The letter indicates if you live on U (the lower floor), K (basement), H (main floor) or L (top floor). Combined with the letter, the first two numbers indicate which floor you live on. The subsequent two numbers indicate your flat number on this floor.
