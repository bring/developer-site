EDI

EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) was started in Bring and the Norwegian postal services in the 90s. On this page you will find EDI guides based on different versions of EDIFACT and XML that is in use today.

Even if Bring and the Norwegian postal services still support older versions of the formats they will not be updated with new changes and services. It is recommendet that everyone either use D04A EDIFACT, XML or the MyBring APIs.

Transport instruction on IFTMIN 04

This guide details how to implement an Bring transport instruction based on IFTMIN D04A EDIFACT. The different documents listed also gives an overview over the diffent services and additional services and how they can be combined in a Bring trasport instruction.
